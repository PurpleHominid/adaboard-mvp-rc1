Welcome to Ada’s MVP Message board (Database Assignment 1 of 2), this is the initial attempt at designing and implementing a basic service to support online discussions between users with similar interests and topics; conversations are packaged and sent via a locally running API before being processed and centrally stored for later retrieval or modification and review. Part of your job will be to help implement a number of basic services; see your backlog for more information.
